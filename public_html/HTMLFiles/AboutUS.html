About the Artist



"Art makes a person breathe with a different kind of happiness". Nice to meet you all, I am Vinita Gohil and I am a graduate. Drawing, painting and sketching are my hobbies and I have been doing them since a very long time. Drawing cartoons, animals, flowers and sketching make me very happy. Drawing is an emotion which encapsulates by emotions and serves as a permanent source of joy. By looking around my environment and people, I get inspired to create these drawings and sketches. I would be delighted to get to know you and take feedbacks from you.
